Parotta

Parotta is a layered flatbread popular in Tamilnadu and Kerala, traditionally made using maida (all-purpose flour). Working with maida and bringing out all the layers while keeping it soft is a tricky process. It involves a lot of elbow grease and the husband happily indulged me by taking over the whole recipe.

ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • salt to taste
  • water
  • milk(if available)

steps

  1. firstly, in a large mixing bowl take 3 cup maida, 2 tbsp rava, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp salt and 2 tbsp ghee.
  2. now add water and start to knead the dough
  3. let it rest for 30 min
  4. make small dough balls
  5. spread the dough and apply oil and flour on the flat dough and make a parotta roll by frills and rotating
  6. now spread the dough again and cook the dough with butter or oil
